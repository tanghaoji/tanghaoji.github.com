Licensing

We would like to give permission to distribute our images under certain conditions, as we believe that art should be shared so that we may inspire others to create their own passions.

There are two types of licensing options that we offer for the use of our photos. The Creative Commons License is the one personal use and it’s for people to share our pictures to others. The Commercial License is for commercial work with our pictures that can generate revenue.

CREATIVE COMMONS LICENSE

This is the most common license that we can out to anyone who would like to use our pictures for personal use.

Everyone is free to…

- Share, copy and distribute our images

- Retouch our images to create a whole new outlook

Conditions: All images must be given credit to Oscar Shi and link to www.mkworkshop.ca. The image cannot be used for commercial use without explicit permission from us.

Use Examples:

- personal use of desktop wallpaper

- an article picture sample describing photography techniques

- blog image, talk about your next visit to Vancouver Island

- Photoshop yourself in the image

COPYRIGHT LICENSE

To use our images for commercial use, you will need our written permission. All commercial uses will require a licensing fee. The fee will be based on the condition of use.

Conditions:

Media type: prints, television, web, posters, newspapers, etc.

Distribution: number of printed copies, exposure level (local, regional, national, international)

Period of use: 6 months, 1 year, 2 years, etc.

Display size: poster size, various page lengths, advertisement size

If there is any licensing inquires please feel free to contact us by email. A representative will speak to you through the phone if necessary.

Use Examples:

- advertisement

- background for a commercial website

- creating prints for distribution
