Affiliate Program

What is our affiliate program?

Our affiliate program allows people who likes our services and would like to inform our services to receive a reward from their heartfelt recommendation.

Who is eligible to participate?

Anyone is allowed to participate.

 

How does it work?

You can spread the word about our services and make sure you keep track of all the people you have made a referral to by recording their first and last name and contact information. Then you can send in your reference list to our representative by phone or email. If the person you have referred made a purchase (ex. bought a print, booked a wedding) through us then you will receive a 10% commission (calculated before taxes) from that purchase.

Ex. Joe told his friends Sally, Bill and Bob about our services and sends us his 3 reference’s information. A few days later Sally and Bob made purchases up to $1000 each. Sally says in her purchase that Joe referenced her but Bob forgot to mention Joe’s reference to us. We will cross examine the connections and Joe will receive $100 from Sally’s purchase and another $100 from Bob’s purchase because Joe sent us his reference list and Bob was on his list. In short, Joe was rewarded $200.

 

How do I get paid?

Commission payment methods are currently only available via PayPal, check and cash. All participates should have a verified PayPal account.

 

F.A.Q. (Frequently Asked Questions)

Can I reference a bunch of random people?

Yes, but your references must have knowledge that you have informed them about Mk.

 

I forgot to give my reference list, will I still receive the commission from my references?

In this situation may still submit your reference list but also if your reference mentioned that you referred them to Mk. you will also get the commission.

 

Can I get paid in cash?

Yes, if you are located in the Greater Vancouver area. Upon your request we will schedule a meeting or make a delivery. (There’s paper to sign when you receive your money!)

 

When will I get paid?

You will receive your payment two weeks (depends on payment methods) after the date of purchase from your references (after cleared transaction).

 

Organization Benefits

We believe in the strength of organizations and their power to change lives of those who need help. So, organizations (ex. non-profit, charities, churches) that have an agreement with us will receive a 15% commission.
